Miguel M. Sandin (PhD)

Exploring eukaryotic ecology and evolution

by understanding environmental molecular diversity

Research interests

I am mainly interested in understanding how and why species interact between them and what factors shaped the diversity we observe today. I am studying environmental DNA, what it represents, its phylogenetic relationships, and its ecological preferences to access known and unknown lineages.
